LAboratory for Treating INformation

LATIN is a laboratory of the Department of Computer Science of the Federal University of Minas Gerais, located in city of Belo Horizonte, state of Minas Gerais, Brazil. The main research interests include the study of problems related to information retrieval in very large full text databases. The main topics are in the areas of indexing, modeling and interfacing.
